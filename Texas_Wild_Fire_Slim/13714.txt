Canto Chamber Choir “In God We Trust”
Sep 11 2011

A remembrance of 9/11 on the 10th anniversary.  At the Marienkirche (Old St. Mary’s Church) at S. Orange and W. San Antonio St.  Adults are $20 each; two or more $15 each.  High school and under $5 each.  A portion of the proceeds goes to Fredericksburg Salutes Our Wounded Warriors.

Address: Old St. Mary's Church at S. Orange and W. San Antonio St.Phone: (830) 997-6523
