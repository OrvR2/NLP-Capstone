   i-Sight Investigation Software on Display at Annual ECOA ConferencePRWeb – 49 mins ago





   Customer Expressions will be on site to showcase i-Sight Investigation Software at the annual Ethics & Compliance Officer Association conference in Bellevue, Washington, from September 20-23rd.Ottawa, Ontario (PRWEB) September 08, 2011  Customer Expressions Corp. (CEC) announced today that the company will be showcasing i-Sight Investigation Software for ethics and compliance investigations at the 19th annual Business Ethics and Compliance Conference, hosted by the Ethics & Compliance Officer Association, in Bellevue, Washington, from September 20-23rd.
The Business Ethics & Compliance Conference is the world’s largest, multi-industry gathering of ethics and compliance officers. Distinguished keynote speakers, leading subject matter experts, and the most prominent ethics and compliance officers in the field will address critical issues facing ethics and compliance practitioners today. 
“Businesses today are dealing with tough new legislation that demands tighter control over their operations. Ethics and compliance departments require affordable, effective case management solutions that make their lives easier,” says Joe Gerard, Vice President at Customer Expressions. 
 “In order for a company to have an effective ethics and compliance program in place, they need to make sure that incidents are thoroughly investigated. i-Sight helps improve the efficiency and quality of investigations because it is built around unique requirements for each client.” says Gerard.
Dawn Lomer, Corporate Journalist at Customer Expressions, will be on-site at the conference to conduct interviews with leading compliance and ethics professionals for the company’s i-Sight Investigation blog and for other industry publications.
The ECOA is a member-driven association serving individuals responsible for developing and maintaining compliance and business conduct programs within their companies. Dedicated to building trust and organizational integrity worldwide, the ECOA is the most respected group of ethics and compliance practitioners in the world. By gathering those new to the field along with seasoned professionals, the ECOA facilitates peer-to-peer learning and helps to foster a global commitment to business ethics and integrity. More than 1,200 ECOA members represent nearly 500 organizations worldwide in more than 30 industries.
About Customer Expressions
Based in Ottawa, Canada, CEC (http://www.customerexpressions.com) is a leading provider of web-based ethics and compliance investigation software. Customer Expressions has gained an international reputation for best-in-class software to optimize the management of various business processes. The privately held firm provides i-Sight, integrated case management software for investigations, complaint handling, corrective and preventive action management (CAPA Management), and other business processes that require case management. For information and breaking news related to ethics and compliance issues and internal investigations, visit our blog at i-sight.com.
