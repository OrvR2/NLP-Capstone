Comedienne Sheryl Underwood sat in with the ladies of The Talk. The subject quickly turned to the suicide death of one of the husbands from The Housewives of Beverly Hills and Sheryl gave a unique and funny opinion. Sheryl recounted that she was happily married and her husband committed suicide in the third year of their marriage. The suicide of her husband was horrific but when the audience moaned Sheryl followed up with a joke, "don't be sad I was acquitted, I had a very good lawyer, don't worry".
