Texas fire destroys 1,554 homes, 17 people missing
September 12th, 2011 | Add a Comment | View Full Content from Source





The number of homes destroyed by a still-raging wildfire here increased on Sunday to 1,554 and will increase further as emergency crews enter areas where the blaze has been extinguished. Seventeen people remain unaccounted for, but officials believe they could simply be out of town.
