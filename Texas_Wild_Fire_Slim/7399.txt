MBA program cut at Miami University

Miami University’s Farmer School of Business will suspend its full-time MBA 
program at the Oxford campus to focus resources on undergraduate teaching 
and its part-time program in West Chester Twp. The accelerated 14-month 
program in Oxford will end after the current class graduates in summer 2012.
