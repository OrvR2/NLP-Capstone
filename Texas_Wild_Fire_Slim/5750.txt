Funerals are a time for reflection, honoring life, and comforting the bereaved -- not club beats and catchy music lyrics. But that won't stop the folks on Twitter from playing a little hypothetical DJ game with the hashtag #inappropriatefuneralsongs.
ACDC's "Highway to Hell" and the always ironic "Stayin Alive" by the Bee Gee's made our list of top song tweets that would make you cringe at a funeral. To check out the rest, scroll through the slideshow below and vote on your favorites.
