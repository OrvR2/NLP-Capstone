Concerns about hotspots in Texas wildfire keep thousands of residents from returning home

Posted on September 11, 2011 by Associated Press 

This article has been syndicated.
BASTROP, Texas — After spending nearly a week wondering whether his home had been destroyed in massive wildfires sweeping across Central Texas, George Gaydos got the news Saturday: His house had burned down in the blaze.
But Gaydos — who has been living at a hotel with his wife, two children and father since fleeing the fires nearly a week ago — still can’t return to his neighborhood to see what is left of his home.
