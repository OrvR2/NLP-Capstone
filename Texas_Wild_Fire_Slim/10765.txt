Potato Salad Recipe
Health | Latest News |	May 30, 2010 at  7:37 pm     









potato salad recipe
1: Potatoes
3 pounds potatoes of your choice: red, Yukon gold, Idaho, hatchery
Scrub, peel if desired, cut and boil in a large pot of water with a pinch of salt until tender. Drain and rinse with cold water. Refrigerate until cold.
2: The Extras
green peppers
pickles
bacon
cheese
cucumbers
2 cups of any of the following:
hard-boiled eggs
celery
onions – red, green, white, yellow
Chop and add the potatoes cool. Mix well.
3: Dressing
mayonnaise
Italian dressing (not creamy optional)
Greek yogurt
cream
2 cups of your choice of binder or a combination:
ranch dressing
2 tablespoons herbs of your choice:
tarragon
coriander
chive
parsley
dill
basil
2 tablespoons all of the following:
vinegar
Dijon mustard
horseradish
lemon juice
lime juice
Salt and pepper
Mix all dressing ingredients choice. Salt and pepper as of taste. Pour over salad mixture of potatoes. Toss to coat.  Refrigerate at least 30 minutes and Eat. Enjoy.
