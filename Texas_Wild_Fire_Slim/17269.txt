Hazmat makes quick work of HCL Spill in Texas
No comments







KCBD Reports: The Lubbock, Texas Fire Department HAZMAT team is assisting the Slaton, Texas Fire Department with a reported hydrochloric acid spill near the railroad in Slaton.
Lubbock County Emergency Management Coordinator Clinton Thetford says crews on scene reported a railroad car containing approximately 30 gallons of the chemical split. We’re told some of the solution is on the ground. Crews are assessing and stabilizing the scene as they await response from railroad response team.
