HOUSTON (KTRK) -- 
A Houston-area hand surgeon will go on trial later today for allegedly assaulting his fourth wife.	
Related ContentStory: Prominent doctor charged with assaulting his wifeMore: Got a story idea?  Let us know!    Doctor Michael Glyn Brown was arrested last August and accused of twisting his fourth wife's arm during an argument at their home.  If convicted, brown faces up to 10 years in prison.  Brown pleaded no contest in 2002 of assaulting his then-pregnant wife with a bed post.
