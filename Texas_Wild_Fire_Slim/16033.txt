        Prime Minister Yousuf Raza Gilani on Sunday reported to have categorically appealed the nation as well as political and democratic forces to support government’s efforts for relief and rehabilitation of the people affected by flood in Sindh.  In his address to the nation on TV and radio, the prime minister urged the international community to extend a helping hand for the flood hit-people. He appealed to political and democratic forces to cooperate with the government in helping the distressed people in Sindh by keep their political differences aside.        1 of 3
