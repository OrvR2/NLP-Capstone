TIMBER DAMAGE FROM HURRICANE IKE









[ Print | 

 ]



September 26, 2008--COLLEGE STATION, Texas--The landfall of Hurricane Ike brought over $350 million in timber damage throughout Texas.  Landowners may contact Texas Forest Service for technical assistance or advice regarding damage to timberland from Hurricane Ike.  You can request this, as well as read the entire timber damage report, at the Hurricane Ike Timber Damage Information page.
