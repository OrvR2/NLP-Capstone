Fire Aid Benefit Concert
Sep 10 2011 to Sep 10 2011

Breaking news on a Rick Trevino-headlined event to benefit victims of the Texas wildfires: There will be a special concert with Trevino and other entertainers this Saturday, September 10 beginning at 4 p.m. at the Fredericksburg KOA east of Fredericksburg at the intersection of Hwy. 290 and FM 1376. Donations for fire victims and fire fighters will be accepted at the event. Concert ticket prices are $15 and are available at the gate or by calling 830-997-4796.  People are encouraged to bring their own coolers and lawn chairs. For updates, visit http://www.facebook.com/ricktrevinoofficial.
 


 


Address: Fredericksburg KOA, 5681 Hwy. 290 E., Fredericksburg, TX 78624
