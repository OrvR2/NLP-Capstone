Today’s individual forest landowner is the future of Texas forestry. Texas forest landowners have plans for their property as varied as the great state in which we live. Texas Forest Service has the professionals who can help develop those plans. They can help landowners practice both traditional and innovative ways to generate income from their property, attract wildlife, develop recreational areas, conserve water and soil and protect their land from wildfire, disease, and erosion.Foresters work with landowners to develop a management or stewardship plan outlining how to achieve specific property goals.If you are interested in exploring forest land management planning or have a specific question about your land, please fill out the downloadable pdf file. Upon receipt of your form, your area Texas Forest Service forester will contact you to schedule a visit to begin drafting your personal property management or stewardship plan. If you would rather not mail or fax your form, you can send an email request to flmplanning@tfs.tamu.edu. Please place in the body of your email the following information: NameStreet AddressCityCountyPhone numberPurpose of your emailIf you have any questions on this process, please call 979-458-6650 or send an email to flmplanning@tfs.tamu.edu.  Thank you.
