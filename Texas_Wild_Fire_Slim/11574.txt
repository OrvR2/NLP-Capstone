Frustration grows for Texas wildfire evacuees

Posted on September 10, 2011 by Christopher Sherman, Associated Press 

This article has been syndicated.						Residents left homeless by a massive Central Texas wildfire turned their attention Friday to what they need to move forward, with some voicing frustration over a perceived delay in federal response even as early signs of recovery appeared in reopened neighborhoods.
