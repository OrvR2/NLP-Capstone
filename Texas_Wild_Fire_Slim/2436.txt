AFFORESTATION PROJECTS









[ Print | 

 ]



To be eligible for the afforestation protocol, landowners must:Have planted forests on or after January 1, 2003Trees must have been planted on land that has been open for the past 10 yearsAgree to not harvest timber for 15 years Enroll all entity owned landsCommit to maintain forest lands for a minimum of 15 years from date of enrollmentCredits are quantified using approved CCX (Chicago Climate Exchange) carbon accumulation tables or through direct measurement (inventory/growth and yield modeling). Upon successful verification, credits are awarded annually based on net growth in sequestered carbon over the previous year.
