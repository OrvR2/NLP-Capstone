StoryText SizeWoman Driving Home Shot, Killed In Downtown OrlandoPassenger Uninjured In Incident, Police SayPOSTED: Thursday, September 15, 2011UPDATED: 6:36 am EDT September 15, 2011 A woman is shot and killed while driving through downtown Orlando.ORLANDO, Fla. -- A woman was shot and killed while driving home early Thursday in downtown Orlando, according to police.The shooting occurred about 2 a.m. at Garland Avenue and Pine Street, near the Orlando Police Department.The woman, whose name has not been released, was shot at least once and was taken to Orlando Regional Medical Center, where she died.A passenger in the car was not injured and was not believed to be involved in the shooting, Orlando police said.After being shot, the woman crashed a few blocks away, near Garland Avenue and Washington Street.Garland Avenue, which was closed while police gathered evidence, reopened about 6:30 a.m.No other information has been released.Watch Local 6 and stay with ClickOrlando.com for more on this story.
