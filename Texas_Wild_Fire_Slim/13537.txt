 Gold medalists of Jamaica team Usain Bolt, Yohan Blake, Michael Frater and Nesta Carter (L to R) react on the podium during the award ceremony for the men's 4x100 metres relay at the IAAF World Championships.
Lee Jae-Won / Reuters





"For me, it was just to go out there fast."
— USAIN BOLT, the world's fastest man, on anchoring Jamaica's 4x100 relay team that set a world record of 37.04 on Sunday in South Korea at the IAAF World Championships (via Sports Illustrated)
