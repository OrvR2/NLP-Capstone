01:10 p.m., Wednesday, September 7, 2011 -- AUSTIN, Texas (AP) — A major wildfire and a temperature inversion combined to bring smoky skies and an air quality advisory to Central Texas.

A thick haze covered much of the Austin and San Antonio area Wednesday morning and ozone levels were expected to reach hazardous levels by afternoon.
