   Phantom Screens wins industry accolade for its website http://www.phantomscreens.comPRWeb – 50 mins ago





   Comprehensive and easy to navigate site wins Crystal Award from industry leading publication Window & Door.Abbotsford, B.C. (PRWEB) September 08, 2011  Phantom Screens has won Window & Door magazine’s 2011 Crystal Achievement Award for Most Innovative Website. 
The corporate website http://www.phantomscreens.com, launched in January 2011, was selected by a panel of judges representing all segments of the industry to recognize significant innovations and achievements in window and door technology, manufacturing, and marketing. 
Jeret Unger, Digital Marketing Manager, welcomed the Crystal Achievement Award, commenting, “Digital marketing is at the core of our future plans and strategies at Phantom Screens. The way people buy products and interact with brands is changing incredibly fast. Here at Phantom Screens we want to be where people are talking about our products and help them with their purchasing journey. http://www.phantomscreens.com is the first step in delivering that quality experience in the online world and it is really exciting to receive an award to acknowledge our hard work.”
The Phantom Screens website was launched following an extensive research program among consumers, specifiers, distributors and sales channel managers. This research resulted in the complete rebuilding and redesigning of the corporate site that educates and informs homeowners, architects, designers and other influencers of the benefits of Phantom’s retractable screen solutions. 
The award-winning website has been designed and engineered in-house and allows for future growth in digital marketing and social media, including online service strategies. 
About Phantom Screens:
Phantom Screens® is North America’s leading provider of retractable screen solutions. Phantom’s screens offer insect protection, solar shading and enhanced privacy for doorways, windows, and large openings such as outdoor living spaces, curtain walls and lift & slide door systems. Phantom retractable screens are designed to pull or lower into place when required and retract out of sight when not in use.
Every Phantom screen is backed with a Limited Lifetime Warranty for added peace of mind. Since its beginning in 1992, the Phantom brand has been synonymous with quality, innovative product development, and superior customer service. According to independent market research, 97% of Phantom customers rate their overall Phantom experience from good to outstanding.
Phantom Screens is a Registered Provider with the American Institute of Architects Continuing Education System (AIA/CES) and AEC Daily, and a member of NCHI and NAHB.
For more information about Phantom Screens retractable screen products, or to find an Authorized Distributor or Dealer near you, please go to http://www.phantomscreens.com or call 1-888-PHANTOM.
