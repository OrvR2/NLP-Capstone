   America’s Next Top Model Looking for New ModelsPRWeb – 8 hrs ago





   Explore Talent Reports that America’s Next Top Model Casting for Cycle 18Los Angeles, CA (PRWEB) September 07, 2011  Explore Talent reported that the CW Television Network started modeling auditions have started for America’s Next Top Model (ANTM).  Any women interested in modeling jobs have to contact an ANTM Casting director for a chance to compete on the show. 
In order to participate, girls have to be between the ages of 18 and 27, be 5’7” tall or higher, and be a citizen of the U.S. The one lucky contestant who will win the ANTM cycle challenge will be awarded with a cash prize, professional management by a modeling agency, and a contract with CoverGirl Cosmetics.
Some of the models trying out for the show find out about the casting through social media websites, such as Facebook, Myspace, Explore Talent, etc. They meet other aspiring models, find out about casting calls and auditions, and, meet photographers and casting agents.
For more information on America’s Next Top Model, go to http://www.exploretalent.com
