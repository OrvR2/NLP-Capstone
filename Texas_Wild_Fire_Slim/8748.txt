Correction: Campbell Soup-Earnings PreviewAP – Thu, Sep 1, 2011





PORTLAND, Ore. (AP) — In a story Aug. 31 that previewed earnings for Campbell Soup Co., The Associated Press reported erroneously that Denise Morrison had been chief operating officer of the company for eight years before becoming CEO. She was an executive with the company for eight years before her appointment, but only served as chief operating officer since 2010.
