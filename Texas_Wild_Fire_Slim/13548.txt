Firefighters progress against Central Texas blaze
Full story: The Atlanta Journal-Constitution

Firefighters are tamping down hotspots and holding back flames from a wildfire that has burned for days across Central Texas, incinerating nearly 1,400 homes and tens of thousands of acres of drought-parched land, officials said Friday.
