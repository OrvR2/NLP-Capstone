Aid Information and Locations

The Minot Disaster Recovery Centers will be open from 7 a.m. until 7 p.m. at the MinotCity Auditorium located at 420 3rd. Ave. SW and at Minot State University's Student Center at 500 University Ave. W.
The Bismarck Disaster Recovery Center will be open from 1 p.m. to 7 p.m. on June 27th, but will operate from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. thereafter. The Bismarck location is at Bank of North Dakota at 1200 Memorial Highway.
The North Dakota Department of Emergency Services encourages homeowners or renters who have received damages from the 2011 flood to report their damages to the state flood damage hotline.
Results will help determine level of FEMA Funding: Information about damages will be collected and used to identify the scale of the flood impact in North Dakota. There are two ways to report damages:  Complete the survey online  Call 1-877-212-0316 to speak with an operator. Callers will be directed through a series of simple questions. The call should only take a few minutes. Hotline hours are 7 a.m. to 7 p.m., Monday through Friday.  Read more:
