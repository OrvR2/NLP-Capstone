   Grandparents Day at Jones Forest





  Sunday, September 11, 2011





Location: Conroe, TexasStart: 1:00 PMEnd: 3:00 PMConnecting the generations by creating memories. An event for grandparents and their grandchildren. Travel through the Jones State Forest and discover native plants, trees and animal identification, forest ecology and much more. Limited to 35 participates. Bring picnic lunch, water and fruit. Starts at 1 p.m. and lasts until 3 p.m. Lead Instructors: John Warner, Anita Ashford, Dawn Vollmer. RSVP: 936/273-2261 ask for Debbie, ext. 200.
