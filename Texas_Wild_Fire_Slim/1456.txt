   Welcome 








Welcome to Unique Vintage! Your source for vintage inspired fashion, from the 1920's flapper era to the 1960's mod looks. We have a wide array of dresses to suit all of your dress needs. We have hand beaded flapper dresses, fringed flapper dresses, 1930's style evening gowns and prom dresses, 1940's Stop Staring and Mad Men style wiggle dresses, 1950's swing dresses and 1960's retro mod go go dresses.

Currently we are featuring homecoming dresses, holiday dresses and casual styles.  We are also seeing the start of our 2011-2012 homecoming dresses in all styles arriving on scene. You'll find short homecoming dresses, cheap homecoming dresses from designers you are sure to fall in love with so if you are looking for something more mature and elegant... We also feature a large selection of little black cocktail dresses, evening gowns, holiday dresses, plus sized dresses and special occasion gowns.
We are proud to introduce once again our incredible selection of unique homecoming dresses at amazing prices. We are also carrying several great designer prom and homecoming dress lines including the 2012 homecoming dresses from Faviana, Mori Lee Prom, Night Moves, Riva, Blush Prom, Black and Pearl by Blush and Terani. This new 2012 line of homecoming dresses is sure to print tons of new looks from short prom dresses, tea length prom dresses, vintage prom dresses, sexy prom dresses and for those of you on a budget, cheap homecoming dresses too!

Thank you for visiting Unique Vintage your source for fabulous vintage inspired clothing!
