 Minot Pet Sheltering Options - Lost Pets 
 Catherine Graham - Monday, June 27, 2011 
 

Pet shelter at North Central Extension Center one mile south of Minot at 5400 Highway 83 S. Service provided by the Souris Valley Animal Shelter. Hours 8 AM to 6 PM.

Pet boarding at Sterling Kennels, 4620 30th Ave Nw, Minot. Call ahead at 701-852-6379.

For pet-friendly lodging, including hotels, motels, bed and breakfasts, cabins and cottages, click HERE.

Have you LOST a pet in the Minot area? Try "Minot LOST PETS" on Facebook.

Also, Minot AFB LOST PETS on Facebook.
