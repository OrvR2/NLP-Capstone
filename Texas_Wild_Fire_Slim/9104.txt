ATTACK FROM THE BLACK

The Attack from the Black training video is available to fire departments across the country. The training video encourages firefighters to think ahead – not just in the moment – while fighting wildfires and stresses the importance of wearing proper Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).The DVD includes fire re-enactment on sand tables, which are proven training tools used by Texas Forest Service. This learning tool helps firefighters think about initial attack strategies, contingency plans and safety zones. Order information:Send an email requesting a DVD and include your name, fire department name and mailing address.
