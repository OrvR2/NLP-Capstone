LONG LIVED WOOD PRODUCTS

 ]



To be eligible for the sustainably managed forest protocol, landowners must: 

Enroll in the sustainably managed forest protocol
Have sustainable forest certifications
Report the quantity and percent of harvested wood in product class categories (pine pulpwood, pine sawtimber, hardwood pulpwood, hardwood sawtimber)

Credits are quantified using CCX (Chicago Climate Exchange) reference tables.  Upon successful verification, credits are awarded based on the amount of carbon stored in these wood products.
