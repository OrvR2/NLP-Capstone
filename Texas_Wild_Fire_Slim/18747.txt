Alabama firefighters to join Texas wildfire battle
Published: Thursday, September 08, 2011,  7:20 AM 


	By 
	
	
	
	
		
			
	
		
			
	
		
	
	
	
	
	Jeremy Gray -- The Birmingham News

GARDENDALE, Alabama -- Two teams of wildland firefighters from the Alabama Forestry Commis­sion will leave today from Gardendale to assist in fighting the Texas wildfire, said commission spokeswoman Co­leen Vansant.The 12 firefighters -- who are tak­ing four bulldozers with them -- will spend two weeks fighting the wildfire, she said.The more than 33,000-acre blaze has blackened about 45 square miles in central Texas, leaving two people dead and consuming more than 1,000 homes.
