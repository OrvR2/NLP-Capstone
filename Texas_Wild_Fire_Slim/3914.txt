June 15, 2007 -- COLLEGE STATION, Texas -- The Attack from the Black training video is now available for Fire Departments.  This training video encourages FD personnel to think ahead and not just in the moment while fighting a fire.  The DVD also stresses the importance of wearing all proper Personal Protective Equipment.Included on this DVD are fires re-enacted on sand tables, which are proven training tools used by Texas Forest Service.  This DVD is a learning tool to help firefighters think about initial attack strategies, contingency plans and safety zonesTo order Attack from the Black, send us an email requesting a DVD and include your name, FD name, and mailing address.  FM 890 Fire                                                            I-40 Fire Shaw Road Fire Tri-County Fire
