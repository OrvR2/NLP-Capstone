 ]



To be eligible for the sustainably managed forest protocol, landowners must: Have sustainable forest certification (SFI, FSC, Tree Farm)   Enroll all entity owned lands  Commit to maintain forest lands for a minimum of 15 years from date of enrollmentCredits are quantified using direct measurement (in field inventory or remote sensing) and growth and yield modeling. Upon successful verification, credits are awarded annually based on net growth in sequestered carbon over the previous year.
