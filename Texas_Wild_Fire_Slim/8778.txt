Analyst Moves: COV, NOKBy MarketNewsVideo | Forbes – Tue, Aug 30, 2011





This morning, Goldman Sachs (GS) increased its EPS estimates on shares of Covidien (COV) as the company plans to buy back more shares of stock. With the higher earnings estimates, Goldman set a new price target of $60 per share and maintained its buy rating.
Shares of Covidien were relatively flat on Tuesday.
And Oppehneimer (OPY) increased its EPS estimates on shares of Nokia (NOK) through 2012 as the company is seeing greater demand from its low-end models. In the report, Oppenheimer maintained its market perform rating.
Shares of Nokia were up 1.62% so far this morning.
