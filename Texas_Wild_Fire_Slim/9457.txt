Published Tuesday, September 13, 2011 12:36 AM


 
Here are the Brazos Valley standouts for Week 2 of the high school football season:
Franklin's Chris Morrow carried 35 times for 430 yards and scored seven touchdowns in the Lions' 70-27 win over the Whitney Wildcats. Teammate Darius Floyd had 14 carries for 121 yards. 
*
In the Battle of the Bell, Rockdale quarterback Ethan Brinkley completed 32 of 51 passes for 300 yards and the Tigers recaptured the bell, 11-10. Cody Skubal caught nine for 114 for the Tigers. Cameron quarterback Jason Kopriva hit 16 of 24 passes for 145 yards. 
*
Burton quarterback Xavier Bethany ran 11 times for 229 yards and two touchdowns and completed 10 of 14 passes for 165 yards and three TDs to lift the Panthers past Louise, 49-8.
*
Brazos Christian running back Jaxton McNair ran 15 times for 229 yards and scored five touchdowns to lead the Eagles over Texas School for the Deaf, 54-29.
*
Dominique Barton carried 14 times for 204 yards, scoring three times. Colin Reed ran 24 times for 116 yards and a touchdown in the Normangee Panthers' 38-21 win over Bremond. Tigers quarterback Jacob White hit 13 of 25 passes for 120 yards and two touchdowns and ran in three conversions.
*
A&M Consolidated's Durasis Nutall had a 90-yard touchdown run, gaining 112 yards on four carries in the Tigers' 39-29 loss at Austin Westlake.
*
Kogan Garrett rushed for 82 yards and completed 15 of 23 passes for 189 yards as Lexington topped Academy 41-20. A.J. Flot had 22 carries for 189 yards. Jason Washington caught 7 passes for 93 yards.
*
Bryan quarterback Chris Johnson ran 13 times for 127 yards and a touchdown and passed for 74 and a score in the Vikings' 35-28 win over Round Rock McNeil.
*
Somerville quarterback Hayden Martinez completed 13 of 18 passes for 326 yards and rushed for 90 yards on 13 carries. Hayden threw for three TDs and ran for three in a 48-12 win against Snook.  Patrick Ward (5-137) and Zach Hawkins (2-142) were the Yeguas' leading receivers.
*
Carter Kacal of Allen Academy rushed for 137 yards and two touchdowns and had a kickoff return to score in a 65-19 win over Harmony Science.
*
Tyler Petersen had 24 carries for 116 yards and a touchdown in Centerville's 26-7 win over Elkhart. 
*
Milano's Chris Juhl carried 14 times for 139 yards and intercepted a pass in the Eagles' 31-16 win over Iola. The Bulldogs' Kyle Bradford completed 10 of 20 passes for 112 yards and two TDs in the loss.
*
Brenham QB Dean Haveman completed 18 of 29 passes for 238 yards, with two touchdowns to Tre'Mund Moore, who caught nine passes for 171 yards. Brenham moved to 3-0 with a 21-10 win over Houston Lamar.
*
Navasota quarterback Kadarius Baker hit 15 of 21 passes for 199 yards and two touchdowns. Solomon McGinty caught them and finished with six receptions for 114 yards in the Rattlers' 31-7 win over Rudder.
*
Madisonville running back Anthony Manns had 98 yards on 15 carries and scored twice in a 51-0 win over Caldwell.
-- DAVID CAMPBELL
