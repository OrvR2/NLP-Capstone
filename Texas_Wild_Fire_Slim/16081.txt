Firefighters have yet to control Texas wildfire

Posted on September 7, 2011 by Jim Vertuno and Michael Graczyk, Associated Press 

This article has been syndicated.						One of the most destructive wildfires in Texas history is slowing down thanks in part to calming winds, but stretched-thin firefighting crews have yet to gain any control of the blaze that is plowing across rain-starved grasslands now littered with hundreds of charred homes.
