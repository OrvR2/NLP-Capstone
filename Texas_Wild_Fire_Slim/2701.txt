In a metropolis as eclectic and stunning as San Francisco, sometimes there's no better date than the city itself. OK, maybe that's just the Carrie Bradshaw in us talking, but still: there's something oddly spectacular about treating yourself to a night on the town. Armed with a book, your iPod or simply some thoughts, next time you're in the mood for a solitary dining adventure, visit one of these five San Francisco restaurants.

Bar Jules is a tiny neighborhood eatery in Hayes Valley. Adored with a sky blue wall, oversized cherry blossom branches, an open kitchen and a cozy bar, it's the perfect spot to pour over your Yoga Journal magazine with a glass of pinot grigio. The lunch and dinner menus change daily guided by the freshest finds at the market. Visit them online to see the today's eats and treat yourself to an intimate date with yourself. 
Bar Jules, 609 Hayes Street at Laguna Street (415-621-5482 or barjules.com)
