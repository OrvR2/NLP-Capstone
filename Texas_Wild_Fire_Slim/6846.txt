2 dead, many missing in Texas wildfires

Tags: US, News, Texas, World, wildfires, Society
 Sep 12, 2011 00:42 Moscow Time



A fire fighting crew from the Lassen National Forest in California clean up hot spots after the destructive wildfire in Bastrop, Texas. Photo: AFP











Two people have died and 17 are missing in a wildfire that has spread across Austin, Texas, destroying about 1,500 homes.The southern US state has been hit by more than 200 wildfires since Monday, prompting President Barack Obama to declare a state-wide state of emergency.Texas Governor and presidential hopeful Rick Perry has cut short his election campaign and returned home to deal with the problem.
