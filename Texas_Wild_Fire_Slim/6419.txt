Can Disneyland ever lose its magic? The institution and brand that's brought us Mickey Mouse and Cinderella, Christina Aguilera and Miley Cyrus is a continued stalwart in Hollywood, producing megawatt stars and generation after generation of wide-eyed fans alike. No wonder Ryan Gosling wooed lady friend Eva Mendes at the golf ball-domed premises recently, following the scores of other famous faces who have joyously romped around with Walt Disney's most memorable characters. Below, we take a look back at the best.
