PlusSouth Florida Fires 'Under Control'South Florida Fires 'Under Control'The Associated PressSouth Florida residents are being warned to stay indoors as smoke from a massive wildfire in and around Everglades National Park continues to burn.  (May 19)Firefighters released this map Monday---an area in South Florida where more than  44-thousand acres have been burning---much of the damage in the Everglades National Park.   [Notes:show map] From the air---you can see the fires are literally eating up the land.... [Notes:dissolve to WSVN aerials]Yet--the good news is --many of the fires are contained at this point... SOT: FLORIDA DIVISION OF FORESTRY SPOKESMAN TODD SCHROEDER."WELL I THINK WE'RE THROUGH THE WORST OF IT. WE'RE IN ACTIVE MOP-UP --PUTTING OUT ANY OF THE HOTSPOTS WE MAY FIND. TRYING TO GET RID OF ALL the SMOKES."THE TOTAL AVERAGE PERCENTAGE CONTAINMENT IS ABOUT 80 PERCENT--WE'RE REALLY GETTING AHOLD OF IT NEW.With 88 fires active over the weekend ----a lot of smoke is hanging over the area ---Residents are being warned to stay indoors ----- and many are grateful they have that option.Firefighters are being praised for saving homes here in Brevard County ---just in the nick of time....  [Notes:show 'thank you' sign] SOT: DAVE GRAY'WE JUST ARE SO GRATEFUL TO THE FIREFIGHTERS THEY SAVED OUR HOUSE AND OUR DOGS....WE'RE SO GRATEFUL'As helicopters drop tons of water on the land below...Residents are doing their part as well.... [Notes:show people clearing brush]SOT: MEGAN NULL" WE'RE GONNA GET ALL THESE TREES CHOPPED DOWN AND ALL THIS BRUSH CLEARED OUT...SO THAT IF IT EVER HAPPENS AGAIN--OUR HOUSE WON'T BURN DOWN....'Weather could be an issue....SOT: FLORIDA DIVISION OF FORESTRY SPOKESMAN TODD SCHROEDER.SOT: WE'RE HOPING FOR SOME RAIN....Rain would be good---but lightning and high winds could help the blazes spread.....SOT: COURSE THAT'S NOT SOMETHING WE CAN CONTROL...BUT W'ERE HOPING FOR THE RAIN..     ___ ___, The Associated Press.(****END****) ANCHOR VOICE:------------------------- VIDEO PRODUCER: Michael Waldon ------------------------------VIDEO SOURCE:
