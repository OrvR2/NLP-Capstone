Texas Forest Service Style Guidelines Texas Forest Service (TFS) Communications has developed the following style guidelines to help you write and edit text and create a high quality, professional visual identity. Our goal is to ensure consistency in agency print and electronic publications, reports, articles, news releases and presentations, particularly those for external audiences.TFS Communications uses the Associated Press Stylebook and Libel Manual and Webster’s Dictionary as primary sources.
