									Thursday, September 8, 2011 - 7:46am							
			

East Texas — 
	Linden-Kildare CISD and Jefferson ISD will be closed again on Thursday due to area wildfires.

	Carlisle ISD classes are back in session.


	Rusk County Sheriff's officials tell KETK, Carlisle ISD has been closed Wednesday morning, due to wildfire nearby.

	The district evacuated students and staff Tuesday afternoon because of the fires.

	Gregg County schools are open for classes, but are being told to keep their kids inside because of the smoke.
	 

	Other schools closed due to wildfire in the area include:

Linden-Kildare CISD and Jefferson ISD.
