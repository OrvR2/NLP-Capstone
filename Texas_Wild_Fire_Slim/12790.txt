XL Insurance, the global insurance underwriting operations of XL Group plc, has promoted Frank Soldano to western regional executive.
In that role, he will lead the business development team and have overall regional management responsibilities. Soldano reports to Uwe Schoberth, head of XL’s Insurance’s sales and regional management team.
Prior to joining Dublin, Ireland-based XL in 1998, Soldano was a regional manager in property and casualty with Zurich Insurance. He was also with INA/Cigna in various management roles. Soldano started his career in Insurance with Allstate in various management roles.
