LEGALS
LEGAL NOTICE
Application has been made for renewal of club registration permit for Eagle’s Nest Private
Club Inc. 102 Crescent View
Dr., Ennis, Texas, Ellis County,
Texas. Said application has
been made to the Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission in accordance with the provision of the Texas Alcoholic
Beverage code. Ray Carlson, President; Lee Gabor, Vice President; and Ginger Cobb, Secretary
LEGAL NOTICE
 PUBLIC FUNDS
 DEPOSITORY
In compliance with the Teas Local Government Code, Chapter 116, the  County of Ellis does hereby solicit applications from county banks for  the period of August 1, 2011 to July 31, 2015 for one or more county  depositories for public funds of the County of Ellis. The Ellis County  Commissioner’s Court will consider a contract for the depositing of  county funds with the selected bank or banks at the regular session of  the Commissioner’s Court of Ellis County, Texas on June 27, 2011 at 6:00  pm in the Ellis County Commissioner’s Courtroom, 101 W. Main St.,  Waxahachie, TX 75165. Bid packets, which are available from the County  Treasurer, are to be submitted to the Purchasing Department, 101 W. Main  St., Suite 304, Waxahachie, TX 75165 by 2:00 pm Tuesday, May 31, 2011  or may be hand delivered to Purchasing before the
due date. A pre-application conference will be held on
Friday, May 13, 2011 at 2:00 pm in the Ellis County Commissioner’s Courtroom, 101
W. Main, 2nd Floor, Waxahachie, TX 75165.
Carol Bush, Ellis County Judge
