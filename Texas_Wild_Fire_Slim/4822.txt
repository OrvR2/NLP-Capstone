SPRING, TX (KTRK) -- 
Classes at Spring High School will resume today after an electrical short caused a small fire yesterday morning	
Related ContentMore: Got a story idea?  Let us know!   The fire started sometime after 8am Wednesday.  School officials had to evacuate the students from the building after a short from the main power line into the building started a fire in the electrical system.  No injuries were reported.  School officials did cancel classes on Wednesday.     Spring High School is expected to hold classes on Thursday after completing repairs on electrical problems that forced the school closure.
