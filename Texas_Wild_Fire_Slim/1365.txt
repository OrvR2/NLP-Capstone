Mexico Sale & New Service from VolarisAirfarewatchdog.com – 16 hrs ago





Beginning in November, look for new service from Mexican low cost carrier Volaris between Chicago Midway and Zacatecas, Mexico City, and Morelia.
Special intro fares are available for travel from November 10 through 20. Tickets must be purchased by September 12.
Other routes are also on sale. Fares include:
Chicago to Mexico City $273 round-trip
Chicago to Morelia $285 round-trip, incl. all taxes
Chicago to Zacatecas $286 round-trip, incl. all taxes
Las Vegas to Guadalajara $460 round-trip, incl. all taxes
