        Silver prices declined 2.01% to USD 41.35 per ounce, on Friday, during the 24 hours ending 23:00GMT, on European debt concerns. In the Asian session at 3:00GMT, silver is trading at USD 41.26 per ounce, 0.22% lower, from 23:00GMT. The first level of short term resistance on the upside is at 42.32, followed by 43.37. The pair is expected to find support at 40.58, with the subsequent support level at 39.89. The white metal is trading below its 20 Hr and its 50 Hr moving averages.        1 of 3
